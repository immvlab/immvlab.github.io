About the Lab

Tsinghua IMMV (Intelligent Manufacturing and Machine Vision) Lab, led by Associate Professor Long Zeng, is an interdisciplinary lab at Institute of Data and Information, Tsinghua University. The lab is dedicated to long-term research on deep learning theories and methods in the field of intelligent manufacturing, with a focus on breakthroughs in key technologies such as embodied intelligent industrial robots, service robots, reconfigurable flexible assembly systems, and intelligent CAD/CAM industrial software. Learn more about our work at official website.
